DonationStation

There are over 20 charities in the Greater Boston area that will send a truck to pick up your donations of used clothing and household items. This app was started in Dec 2014 to help charities admins manage their donation pickup programs (track, schedule, download and map out confirmed pickups with a custom dashboard). Donors can also use the app to schedule a donation to a charity of their choice and confirm via email. Find it on heroku. Demo admin credentials available by request.
